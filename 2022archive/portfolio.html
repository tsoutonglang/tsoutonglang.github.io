I've been making and posting my art under the name SpaceBunny.exe since the summer of 2018, although I've been creating art since I was young. I'm a self taught digital illustrator and I'm currently learning how to create visual novel games with Ren'Py and how to animate. Most of my work has been fan art based off of shows I've watched and games I've played, each made with the intent of letting my emotions and thoughts out.

I joined Manga Art Studio in April 2020. Since then I've been consistently creating art every week and have seen improvement both in technique and style between when I first started to where I am today. Currently we are learning how to clean and color animation frames to create an original video animation.

I hope you enjoy my art!
